What is SafeCare?

SafeCare is an additional layer of support that is exclusively available to all schools who use the SafeCare for Education online learning service. There is no additional charge to customers for using this service, it is just part of our commitment to keep our customers up-to-date and help keep everyone safe. It comprises a bank of online safeguarding and wellbeing support resources that can be shared with, and have been written specifically for, both parents/carers and young people.
